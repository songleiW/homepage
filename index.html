Biography

I am a fourth year computer science and technology Ph.D. student at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen where I am advised by Xiaohua Jia and Yifeng Zheng. My research interests lie in applied cryptography, focusing on secure multi-party computation, differential privacy, privacy-preserving machine learning, as well as searchable encryption.
