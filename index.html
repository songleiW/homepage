Biography

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, under the supervision of Xiaohua Jia and Yifeng Zheng. My research interests lie in applied cryptography, focusing on secure multi-party computation, differential privacy, privacy-preserving machine learning, as well as searchable encryption.

I earned a B.E. degree in the Internet of Things from China University of Petroleum (East China) in 2018 and an M.E. degree in Computer Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, in 2021.
