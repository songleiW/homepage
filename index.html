Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor (“Hundred Talents Program”) in the National Engineering Laboratory for Big Data System Computing Technology, Shenzhen University. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, under the supervision of Xiaohua Jia and Yifeng Zheng. Before that, I obtained my Master degree in Computer Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, under the supervision of Xiaohua Jia and Jiangang Shu, and obtained my Bachelor degree in Internet of Things from China University of Petroleum (East China).

I focus on utilizing secure multi-party computation techniques (such as secret sharing, differential privacy, etc.) to address various practical problems (such as privacy-preserving graph analysis and search, privacy-preserving machine learning, etc.).
