Biography

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, under the supervision of Xiaohua Jia and Yifeng Zheng. I obtained my Master degree in Computer Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen in Jan. 2021, under the supervision of Xiaohua Jia and Jiangang Shu, and obtained my Bachelor degree in Internet of Things from China University of Petroleum (East China), in July 2018.

I focus on utilizing secure multi-party computation techniques (such as secret sharing, differential privacy, etc.) to address various practical problems (such as privacy-preserving graph analysis and search, privacy-preserving machine learning, etc.).
